# Weekly App Launch Research Packet

## App Snapshot
- App name: SignalSprout
- One-liner: A lightweight app that helps ambitious people turn goals into daily systems
- Launch stage: prelaunch waitlist growth

## Audience Pains
- People set goals but fall off because their daily systems are vague
- Productivity apps often feel bloated and overwhelming
- Users want discipline and consistency without spending all day managing tools
- Many people consume motivational content but still fail to act consistently

## Competitor Patterns
- Most apps sell generic productivity instead of a narrow outcome
- Many brands over-explain features before proving user value
- Faceless app pages often perform better when they repeat a single strong problem statement

## Hook Ideas
- "Most people do not have a motivation problem. They have a system problem."
- "Why your goals keep dying after 3 days"
- "This is what most productivity apps get wrong"
- "We are building the tool we wish existed for consistency"
- "If you want discipline, your environment needs to help"

## Objections To Answer
- "Why not just use Notes or Reminders?"
- "Is this just another habit app?"
- "What makes this different from other productivity tools?"
- "Will this actually help me stay consistent?"

## Topics To Cover This Week
- Problem-first videos about why goals fail
- Build-in-public updates
- Simple feature reveals tied to user outcomes
- Faceless reels that turn pain points into curiosity
- Waitlist conversion posts

## CTA Goal
- Push users to join the waitlist and pre-download when available
